About "My Weather"
"My Weather" is a way to use your browser to check current weather patterns by using names of different cities.
The app can forecast for every longitude and latitude point on Earth with Superior Accuracy

Enter the name of the city in the provided search bar.

Click on "Get Weather" button to get weather details.
